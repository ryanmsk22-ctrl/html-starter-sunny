Strategic Marketplace Partner

01

Model 02

Authorized Retail Distributor

02

We become your authorized distributor — purchasing inventory directly and managing the full retail relationship on your behalf. This protects your brand equity, eliminates grey-market risk, and gives you a high-performance sales channel with zero operational overhead.

What's included
  • Direct inventory purchase — we carry the capital risk
  • Authorized seller status that protects your brand integrity
  • Proactive unauthorized seller removal & MAP enforcement
  • Full logistics, FBA prep & inbound fulfilment management
  • Amazon Brand Registry management & brand health monitoring
  • Transparent reporting on all sales, inventory & returns

Model 03

Inventory & Wholesale Partner

03

We purchase your inventory at wholesale, deploying our own capital so you don't have to wait for sales to fund growth. This model is ideal for brands that want guaranteed volume, a reliable purchase order partner, and zero marketplace fulfilment headaches.

What's included
  • Guaranteed purchase orders — our capital, your growth
  • Wholesale pricing agreements & volume commitments
  • FBA prep, labelling, bundling & inbound logistics
  • Inventory forecasting, demand planning & reorder management
  • Zero stockout guarantee on all managed SKUs
  • Full sales velocity reporting by ASIN & marketplace
